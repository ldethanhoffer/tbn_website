    About

    Toronto Bi+ Network (TBN) is a welcoming community for bisexual, pansexual, omnisexual, Two-Spirit, fluid, unlabeled, and other non-monosexual (bi+/mspec) people, as well as anyone questioning their sexuality.

    We offer peer support, social connection, information, and resources. Our spaces are non-judgmental and supportive, so people can share feelings, experiences, and concerns openly. We celebrate many paths of identity development and honour the diversity of people's lived experiences. We also do outreach, advocacy, and education on bi+ and broader queer issues.

    TBN also serves as an umbrella organization for two gender-centered groups: Bi+ Women of Toronto (BiWoT) and Bi+ Men of Toronto (BiMoT).

    Bi+ Women of Toronto (BiWoT)

    Bi+ Women of Toronto (BiWoT) is a community where bi+ women and women questioning their sexuality can share perspectives and experiences. We welcome bi+ and questioning cis and trans women, as well as non-binary people who are comfortable in a space centered on women's experiences.

    Bi+ Men of Toronto (BiMoT)

    Bi+ Men of Toronto (BiMoT) is a community where bi+ men and men questioning their sexuality can share perspectives and experiences. We welcome bi+ and questioning cis and trans men, as well as non-binary people who are comfortable in a space centered on men's experiences.
